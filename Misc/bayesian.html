Introductory Review of (some) Bayesian Statistics

Based on Kruschke's Doing Bayesian Data Analysis

October 17, 2022 Carlos

As part of my new research work, I need to have a very good understanding of Bayesian statistics, which I have not had exposure to before. For that reason, I wrote up this document going over some introductory facets of Bayesian statistics. It's very incomplete, of course, but it's a useful tool for brushing up.

I made this document myself, but I wouldn't call it my own original work, as I am walking through Kruschke's "Doing Bayesian Data Analysis" for all of this documents. These are more my notes on the topic than any original findings.
