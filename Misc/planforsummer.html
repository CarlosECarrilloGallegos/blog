Plan for the Summer!

What I'll be working on at the Recurse Center

Juen 23, 2022 Carlos

This summer I'm fortunate enough to be working with a ton of great programmers at the Recurse Center. My goal is to improve as a programmer as much as possible while working on things I like. I have three main projects I want to get started, and see what keeps my interest after that.

What I'm probably most interested in is applying machine learning to geospatial data. I have a ways to go in this respect, as my experience with ML is fairly minimal. As the weeks go on, I'll be sure to narrow the scope of this project. The first goal is to get my head around ML fundamentals, and keep learning by working on this project.

Next, I really want to learn Julia. I've been meaning to for a while, but haven't come around to it. I want to build on my knowledge of applied numerical methods from a course I took in MATLAB through Julia.

Lastly, I want to go over various other subjects. I'm interested in building a better understanding of Linear Algebra, and maybe picking up a bit of R and SQL. We'll see how this all goes, though!
